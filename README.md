### About the Temple | ఆలయం గురించి
This website is dedicated to Sri Lakshmi Chennakesava Swamy Temple, 
an ancient Vijayanagara era temple built in **1513 AD** at Shotrium Gundala village.

The temple stands as a symbol of faith and devotion for the people of 
Gundala and surrounding villages for over **512 years**.
